3. Verarbeitung besonderer Datenkategorien (Gesundheitsdaten)

Wichtig: Daten wie Körpergewicht, Größe, Ernährung, Schlafdauer und Stresslevel gelten als Gesundheitsdaten im Sinne von Art. 9 DSGVO. Diese werden nur mit Ihrer ausdrücklichen Einwilligung verarbeitet.

Vor Abschluss des Abonnements bestätigen Sie im Onboarding per Checkbox ausdrücklich Ihre Einwilligung zur Verarbeitung dieser Daten zum Zwecke der Erstellung Ihres personalisierten Plans. Wir dokumentieren die Einwilligung mit Zeitstempel.

Rechtsgrundlage: Art. 9 Abs. 2 lit. a DSGVO (ausdrückliche Einwilligung).

Widerruf: Sie können Ihre Einwilligung jederzeit widerrufen — per E-Mail an info@mj-performance.net oder durch Kündigung Ihres Abonnements. Nach Widerruf werden Ihre Gesundheitsdaten gelöscht, sofern keine gesetzlichen Aufbewahrungspflichten bestehen.

Keine automatisierte Einzelentscheidung: KI-generierte Empfehlungen sind keine automatisierten Einzelentscheidungen i.S.v. Art. 22 DSGVO. Sie dienen als individueller Vorschlag und ersetzen keine medizinische Beratung.

2. Data collection in the PEAK app

During onboarding (before registration)

  • Personal information: first name, age, gender, height, current weight, target weight, sleep duration
  • Lifestyle: job type, commute, stress level
  • Sports and fitness: sport, experience level, training frequency, training duration, training environment
  • Nutrition: allergies, dietary style, preferred cuisines, cooking skills, weekly budget
  • Goals: desired fitness/nutrition goal

During registration and payment

  • Email address
  • Payment and billing data (processed directly by Stripe)
  • Consent record (timestamp)

During use

  • Usage data, logged meals, training sessions, weight changes
  • Technical data (IP address, browser, device type)

Legal bases: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract), Art. 6(1)(a) + Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR (consent for health data), Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest).

3. Special categories of data (health data)

Important: Data such as body weight, height, nutrition, sleep duration and stress level qualify as health data within the meaning of Art. 9 GDPR. Processed only with your explicit consent.

Before subscribing, you confirm your explicit consent via checkbox during onboarding. We document consent with timestamp.

Legal basis: Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR (explicit consent).

Withdrawal: You can withdraw your consent at any time by email to info@mj-performance.net or by cancelling your subscription.

No automated decision-making: AI-generated recommendations do not constitute automated individual decisions under Art. 22 GDPR. They serve as suggestions and do not replace medical advice.

4. Payment processing

We use Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd. (Dublin, Ireland). Transmitted: name, email, billing address, phone, payment data. MJ Performance has no access to full payment data.

Privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR. US transfer via EU-US DPF + SCC.

5. Authentication

Magic Link (email login)

To log in, you receive a one-time link by email. We process your email and a cryptographic token.

Google Sign-In (OAuth, optional)

When used, Google transmits to us: email, name, profile picture (if available), Google ID. Google is an independent controller. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Auth infrastructure: Supabase

Managed via Supabase Inc. (Singapore / EU servers in Frankfurt). Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (Magic Link), Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (Google OAuth).

6. AI processing

For generating personalised plans we use Anthropic PBC (San Francisco, USA).

Only data necessary for plan generation is transmitted: first name (for personalised salutation in the plan), basic demographics (age, gender, height, weight), lifestyle information, sport and training data, dietary preferences, allergies and your goals. Last name, full email address, phone number and payment data are not transmitted.

Anthropic processes this data as a processor and does not use it to train their models.

Privacy policy: anthropic.com/legal/privacy

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) + Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR. US transfer via SCC + DPA.
